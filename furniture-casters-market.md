<p><p>The furniture casters market is primarily divided into two main types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating wheel that allows for easy maneuverability, making them ideal for mobile furniture like office chairs and carts. In contrast, rigid casters have a fixed wheel that provides stability and straight-line movement, suitable for heavier items or stationary applications. Both types enhance functionality, offering options tailored to specific furniture needs and user preferences in various environments.</p></p>
