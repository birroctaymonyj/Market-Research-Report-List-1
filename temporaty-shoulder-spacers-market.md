<p><strong>The Temporaty Shoulder Spacers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cementless</li><li>Cemented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temporary shoulder spacers market is divided into two primary types: cementless and cemented. Cementless spacers use a design that fosters biological fixation, promoting bone integration without the need for cement, making them easier to remove later. In contrast, cemented spacers are bonded to the bone with a medical-grade cement, offering stability and immediate fixation. Both types cater to specific clinical needs during infection management or joint replacement surgeries, balancing performance, ease of use, and patient outcomes.</p></p>
