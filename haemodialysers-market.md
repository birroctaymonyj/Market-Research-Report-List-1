<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The haemodialysers market is categorized into two main types: low flux membranes and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes are designed to effectively remove small molecules and toxins, making them suitable for patients with stable kidney function. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for the removal of larger molecules and inflammatory substances, offering enhanced clearance for patients with more severe renal failure. These differences in membrane capabilities cater to varying patient needs and treatment efficacy in dialysis procedures.</p></p>
