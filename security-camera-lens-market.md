<p><p>The security camera lens market consists of three primary types: fixed lens, varifocal lens, and zoom lens. Fixed lenses have a set focal length, providing a consistent field of view suitable for specific monitoring areas. Varifocal lenses allow for adjustable focal lengths, enabling users to modify the field of view without changing the camera. Zoom lenses offer variable focal lengths with the ability to magnify or reduce images remotely, making them ideal for diverse surveillance needs. Each type serves distinct security requirements.</p></p>
