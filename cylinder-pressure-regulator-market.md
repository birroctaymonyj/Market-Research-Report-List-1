<p><p>The cylinder pressure regulator market is divided into three main types based on pressure levels: low pressure regulators, medium pressure regulators, and high pressure regulators. Low pressure regulators maintain a steady output pressure for applications like home heating. Medium pressure regulators are used in industrial settings where moderate pressure control is needed for processes like gas distribution. High pressure regulators manage significant pressure drops in high-demand environments, ensuring safe and efficient operation in sectors such as welding and gas transportation.</p></p>
