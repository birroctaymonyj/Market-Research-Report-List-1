<p><p>Automatic leak test apparatus is categorized into two primary types: semi-automatic and full automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention during the testing process, involving operator input for setup or monitoring, making them suitable for lower volume applications. In contrast, full automatic systems operate independently, managing the entire testing cycle without human oversight, which enhances efficiency and consistency. Full automatic systems are typically favored in high-volume production environments where speed and accuracy are critical.</p></p>
