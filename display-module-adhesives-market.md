<p><ul><li>UV Curable Type</li><li>Non UV Curable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Display Module Adhesives market is categorized into UV Curable and Non-UV Curable types. UV Curable adhesives cure rapidly upon exposure to ultraviolet light, providing quick processing and strong bond formation suitable for various displays, including LCDs and OLEDs. In contrast, Non-UV Curable adhesives rely on heat or chemical reactions for curing, offering flexibility in applications where UV exposure is impractical. Both types are essential in ensuring durability and performance in electronic displays while catering to specific manufacturing needs.</p></p>
