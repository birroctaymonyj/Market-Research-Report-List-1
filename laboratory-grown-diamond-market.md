<p><ul><li>CVD Diamonds</li><li>HTHP Diamonds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory-grown diamond market primarily consists of two main types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HPHT (High-Pressure High-Temperature) diamonds. CVD diamonds are produced by depositing carbon gases in a controlled environment, allowing for precise growth and fewer impurities. In contrast, HPHT diamonds replicate the natural diamond formation process by applying high pressure and temperature to carbon. Both types offer consumers flawless, ethically sourced alternatives to natural diamonds, increasingly appealing to environmentally-conscious buyers in the jewelry market.</p></p>
