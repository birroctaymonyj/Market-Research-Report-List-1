<p><p>In-plant logistics in the automobile OEM market encompasses three key types: in-plant warehousing, line-side feeding, and packing. In-plant warehousing involves storing components and materials within the manufacturing facility to streamline inventory management and enhance efficiency. Line-side feeding refers to delivering parts directly to the assembly line at the optimal time, minimizing delays. Lastly, packing involves preparing and organizing finished products for shipment, ensuring they are adequately protected and ready for distribution, ultimately supporting a seamless production process.</p></p>
