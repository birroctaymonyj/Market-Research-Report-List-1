<p><p>Automatic marking systems consist of various technologies, primarily laser and inkjet marking. Laser marking uses focused light to create permanent marks on surfaces, offering high precision and durability. It's commonly used in industries requiring robust and long-lasting identification. Inkjet marking, on the other hand, utilizes liquid ink to print information, allowing for flexibility and speed, ideal for high-volume production. Each technology serves different applications, with laser systems excelling in durability and inkjet in versatility and throughput.</p></p>
