<p><p>The Process Gas Chromatographs market encompasses various types, including Gas-Solid Chromatography (GSC) and Gas-Liquid Chromatography (GLC). GSC involves the separation of gaseous mixtures based on their interactions with a solid stationary phase, making it suitable for applications like analyzing trace gases. GLC, on the other hand, uses a liquid stationary phase to separate volatile compounds in gaseous samples, widely employed in chemical analysis, environmental monitoring, and petrochemical industries. Both methods are essential for precise gas composition analysis.</p></p>
